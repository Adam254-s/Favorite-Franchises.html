Top Favorite Franchises That I Love!

Throughout my life there were so many franchises that I loved and grew up with that I'd basically wouldn't know what my life would've been without them. So here are some of my favorite franchises that I still love to this day.

Sonic The Hedgehog

This blue hedgehog has been around in my life ever since I was in kindegarden. I loved Sonic so much and yet despite it's terrible decade which was the 2010's. The franchise now looks like it's thriving once again. Now, I'm starting to become more of a Sonic fan than a Mario fan because as I got older, I feel like the series has also grown up on me as well.

Yu-Gi-Oh

I really wish that I saw this series more when I was a kid, but I couldn't find it anywhere except on Nick Toons. When I finally watched this series. I fell in absolute love with it. I just love the cards and the characters portrayed in this franchise. Yes, modern Yu-Gi-Oh is ridiculous with all of it's insane combo decks but just like Pokemon, This series will always have a big place in my heart. My favorite series being 5d's.

Pokemon

Pokemon has been a huge impact on my life my first gen being gen 4 and gen 5 being my absolute favorite generation based on it's characters, the story, and the Pokemon overall, Hyderigon best dragon type pokemon baby!

Mario

I love Nintendo games I HATE Nintendo as a company. No suprises there since they do make memorable games and characters. But, this franchise still has a big special place in my heart because this franchise is the og of platforms. I still gotta respect Mario even if the franchise is getting a little stale with the way mario games are.

Dragon Ball

Dragon ball was the hypest thing I've ever witnessed when I was a little kid, I remember imitading what Dragon Ball Characters do because I loved the way they powered up and used their special attacks. Playing the new game as if me typing this and I don't care what people have to say, it's my favorite fighting game of all time. Sparking Zero is just all hype although I do understand people's complaints about it. Dragon Ball has been a big part of my childhood and I enjoyed every second of it. My favorite saga being both the Android saga and Cell saga. Trunks, Gohan, and Cell were beasts!

Teen Titans

Teen Titans was my first DC animated show that I watched when I was little. I remember loving watching this show and just loving everything on what the characters did and went through. My favorite episode being never more and seeing Robin overcoming slade and beating him. I wish this show would come back for one more season just to have a satistying feeling. Raven best Titan easily. Always loved her since she was my favorite and her battle with Trigon was very emotional and epic.
